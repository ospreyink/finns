The historic Finn's Restaurant—established in 1952 and formerly located at the top of Parley's Way in Salt Lake City—returned the summer of 2006. The new Finn's Cafe is operated by the same Gurholt family as Finn's Restaurant with Finn Gurholt as proprietor, chef and baker. Finn will provide the food and excellent service you remember with an updated menu in a casual modern cafe setting.

The newly renovated building can accommodate up to 110 guests. The inside seating area with its warm colors, wood floors, and ceilings, is the perfect place for up to 50 guests in the summer or winter seasons. In the warmer months we invite you to enjoy our 60 seat outdoor patio, a wonderful place to taste our seasonal menu created from the freshest possible ingredients.

Finn's Cafe features an espresso bar with a bakery giving customers the chance to enjoy Little Finn's sourdough breads and pastries.
